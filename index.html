Logofolio'22
rhetoric as a discipline

My strengths are in theory, criticism and analysis. I work on the construction of the discourse of each project with a methodology based on rhetoric. Discipline that is made up of closely interrelated processes:

Intellectio

Previous stage.
Where the problem is understood.

Inventio

1st stage.
Where ideas are sought and created.

Dispositio

2nd stage.
Where the ideas are arranged in the message.

Elocutio

3rd stage.
Where communication is articulated linguistically and visually.

Memoria

4th stage.
Where the actions are memorized prior to their presentation.

Actio

5th stage.
Where the message is finally transmitted to the target audience.
